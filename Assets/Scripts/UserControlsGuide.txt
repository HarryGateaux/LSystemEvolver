Controls for the GUI

Main GUI

Left click on image to add to selection
Left click on image caption to zoom in and inspect
Right click to clear selection
Space bar to reset the application to beginning
Esc to quit in built version
Click on Evolve to generate next generation

Zoomed in view

Rotation:

Hold mouse wheel and move mouse
Arrow keys

Zoom:

Mouse Scroll Wheel to zoom in/out
w - zoom in
s - zoom out
r - toggle animation of mesh on / off
t - complete the mesh (if animation toggled off)
